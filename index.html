Projects

Multi-Condition Synthetic Driving Dataset for Instance Segmentation
CARLA + GroundedSAM annotation pipeline · May – Jun 2025
collaborative
  • Designed and built the full dataset: scripted four environmental conditions (day, night, rain, fog) in the CARLA simulator, synchronizing a dual-camera RGB + instance-segmentation rig at 20 FPS and converting raw contours into YOLO-format polygons — 3,704 frame pairs covering vehicles, pedestrians, buses, and traffic lights (~10.2K / 1.9K / 2.2K instances).
  • Built a GroundedSAM-based auto-labeling pipeline (grounded-language prompts → SAM masks) to refine annotations where the simulator's native ground truth fell short, plus position-based train/test splitting for true spatial generalization and an Albumentations augmentation stage.
  • Delivered the dataset to a collaborator who trained YOLOv11n-seg and YOLOv11m-seg and compiled the nano model to a TensorRT (FP16) engine, cutting inference latency ~4.1× (11.87ms → 2.89ms; 84 → 346 FPS).
github.com/Mound21k/yolov11-carla-segmentation
